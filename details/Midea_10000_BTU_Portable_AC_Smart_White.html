Midea 10000 BTU Portable Air Conditioner

Enjoy smart and efficient cooling with the Midea 10000 BTU portable air conditioner. Perfect for rooms up to 300 square feet, this unit ensures a cool and refreshing environment in no time. Designed to handle your needs, it offers cooling, dehumidification, and fan-only modes to provide ultimate versatility for any situation, whether you're looking to combat heat, reduce humidity, or simply keep the air moving.

With the Midea SmartHome app, you can control your portable AC remotely from your smartphone, offering convenience and peace of mind. Not only is it easy to use, but it also supports voice commands through Alexa and Google Assistant, making it a smart addition to your home or office.

This compact unit is ideal for apartments, seasonal use, or when you need extra cooling in a specific room. Included in the package is a 5ft exhaust hose and window brackets, so setup is quick and hassle-free. The digital LED display provides real-time information, and the compact remote control, with batteries included, lets you manage the unit without any extra effort.

Enhance your air quality with the removable washable air filter, which captures dust, pet hair, and other impurities, ensuring cleaner and healthier air for you and your family.
